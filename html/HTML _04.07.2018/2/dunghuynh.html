I got the panna cotta milk tea with 100% sugar and loved it! It's really not too sweet at all, and if you are unsure, the owner is more than willing to give you a sample to try.

OLA COFFEE

OLA's was founded in Silicon Valley in 2004 led by Dr. Ola Robert Hassan. Together with scientist friends, Dr. Ola went to all coffee growing regions around the globe to study the genetic makeup of coffees and why coffees are so acidic. What I found and created through process, soil, harvesting and growing is no to low acidic coffees from Africa with premium grade selections.
